Bio

I am Chenchen YU. Now I work Lenovo Research as a pattern recognition researcher. I got my bachelor and master degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2015 and 2018 respectively. I once worked in Didi chuxing(the leading one-stop mobile transportation platform in China) and Baidu tech(the biggest search company in China) as a student intern.

My research direction is machine learning, deep learning, speech enhancement, acoustic sound event detection, etc.
